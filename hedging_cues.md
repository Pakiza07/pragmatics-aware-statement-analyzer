# Hedging Cues (v0.1)

## 1. Modal Verbs
- may
- might
- could
- would

## 2. Verbs of Cognition / Belief
- think
- believe
- suggest
- appear
- seem

## 3. Adverbs of Uncertainty
- possibly
- probably
- potentially
- generally
- often
- sometimes
- likely
  
## 4. Quantifiers
- some
- many
- a bit
- kind of

## 5. Phrasal Constructions
- it seems that
- there is a chance that
- tend to

## 6. Epistemic Hedges
- All I know is
- I'm not sure if
- From what I was told
  
  **Important note:** Context matters, not every hedging term comes in the form of a hedge.
